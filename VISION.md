# FXD Vision

FXD is an AI-powered engineering platform for designing industrial fixtures, beginning with welding fixtures for fabricated assemblies.

FXD is not intended to replace CAD software. It acts as an intelligent engineering system that analyzes assemblies, understands fabrication intent, and generates editable fixture concepts that engineers can review, modify, and approve.

## Mission

The software should think like an experienced fixture engineer.

## Long-term direction

FXD will remain CAD-neutral, begin with STEP-based workflows, and expand from weld fixturing into assembly fixtures, inspection fixtures, robotic tooling, and other manufacturing applications.
