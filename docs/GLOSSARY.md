# FXD Glossary

This glossary keeps engineering, software, and AI agents using consistent terminology.

## Terms

### Build orientation
The selected position of the product during fixturing and manufacturing.

### Clamp
A device that applies force to hold a component against established locators or supports.

### Datum
A theoretically exact reference used to establish location or orientation.

### Fixture concept
A complete proposed locating, supporting, clamping, access, and base-structure strategy.

### Hard stop
A fixed physical feature that establishes a component position in a defined direction.

### Locator
A feature that establishes component position by contacting a permitted surface, edge, or hole.

### Overconstraint
A condition where redundant restraints conflict with part variation, tolerance, or thermal movement.

### Support pad
A contact feature that carries weight or process load without necessarily locating every degree of freedom.

### Unloadability
The ability to remove the completed assembly from the fixture without collision, disassembly, or damage.

### Weld access
The geometric and process clearance required for the torch, electrode, operator, robot, shielding, and joint approach.
