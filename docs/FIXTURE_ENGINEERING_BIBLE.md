# FXD Fixture Engineering Bible

This is the practical knowledge base for experienced fixture-engineering judgment.

It should capture lessons such as:

- what works in real production
- what looks correct in CAD but fails on the floor
- loading and unloading mistakes
- clamp and locator failures
- weld-access and spatter problems
- distortion lessons
- operator and robot access
- maintainability and wear
- when laser-cut construction is appropriate
- when machined or purchased components are justified

## Entry format

- lesson
- manufacturing context
- why it matters
- bad outcome prevented
- exceptions
- supporting example or evidence

Do not place customer-identifying information, employer CAD, confidential drawings, or proprietary geometry in this public repository.
