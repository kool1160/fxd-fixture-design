# FXD Product Principles

## Mission

FXD is a digital fixture engineer. Its purpose is to help engineers create practical, manufacturable fixtures while preserving engineering judgment and keeping the human engineer in control.

## Principles

- AI proposes. Engineering validates.
- Never modify the customer's original CAD.
- Every recommendation must be explainable.
- Manufacturing practicality takes priority over geometric perfection.
- Fixtures should control parts without unnecessary overconstraint.
- Preserve weld, operator, robot, maintenance, and unload access.
- Prefer standard components before custom components.
- Multiple valid fixture concepts may exist.
- Engineering assumptions must remain visible and editable.
- Learn from engineers without silently turning preferences into universal rules.
- Keep the engineering core CAD-neutral.
- Think like an experienced fixture engineer.
